04 — About

A developer who thinks in interfaces, motion and play.

That overlap is the point. Years of design work mean I can own an interface, not just implement a spec. Game development keeps my instinct for motion, feedback and feel honest.

For development roles, read the work top to bottom; it's engineering first. The design and game projects are there to show how I think.
